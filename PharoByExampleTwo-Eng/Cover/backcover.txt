Pharo is an object-oriented programming language. Largely due to its expressivety and simplicity, the community around Pharo has been steadily increasing. Pharo leverages the software building experience to its best by offerring open and object-oriented programming environments and libraries.

This book covers advanted features and libraries of the Pharo language. Innovative and widely used frameworks are also described in a tutorial-like fashion.

Programmers familiar with Pharo will enjoy the highlights made of some particularly beautiful aspects of Pharo. Practicioners making their debut with Pharo will board for a wonderful journey in the realm of objects. 

Deep into Pharo is a book about beauty of object-oriented designs. Pharo's purety in its principles and its object model turn what we wanted to be a reference to the Pharo community into a book about computational Art.
